Notes on Camera Standards

Most consumer-grade cameras and tripods come fitted with a 1/4"-20 UNC thread,
including most DSLR cameras.
Larger and heavier cameras, pro-cameras and lens mounts may use a 3/8"-16 UNC thread.
Both these standard thread sizes are a part of the ANSI standard.
